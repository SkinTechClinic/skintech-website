— Common questions

Clarity, first.

A few of the questions we hear most often. If yours isn't here, reach out — we'd rather you ask.

How many laser hair removal sessions will I need?

Most clients require 6–8 sessions spaced 4–6 weeks apart for the face and 6–8 weeks for the body. Hair grows in cycles, and lasers only affect actively growing hair — so patience is part of the protocol. Maintenance sessions once or twice a year help sustain results.

Is the treatment suitable for my skin type?

Yes. The GentleMax Pro Plus platform is calibrated for Fitzpatrick skin types I through VI, including deeper and more reactive tones. Every session begins with a skin read and a customised setting — we do not use a single default protocol.

How should I prepare for my first session?

Avoid direct sun and self-tanner for two weeks, do not wax or pluck the area for four weeks, and shave 24 hours before your appointment. Come with clean skin — no lotion or deodorant on the treated area. We send a full pre-care note when you book.

What does aftercare look like?

Expect mild warmth or redness for a few hours. Apply SPF 50 for at least a week, skip saunas, intense exercise and hot showers for 24 hours, and avoid active ingredients (retinol, acids) for 3–5 days. Hydrate generously, inside and out.
